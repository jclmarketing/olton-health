Why Sheldon chooses Olton Health

Just down the A45 — handy for patients near Birmingham Airport, NEC and the Coventry Road.

We've welcomed patients from Sheldon (B26) since opening in 2014. Whether you've been pushed past breaking point at your desk, can't shake a sports injury, or want to stay strong and moving as you get older, we'll work out what's going on and what'll fix it — without keeping you in our diary longer than you need.

Services we offer Sheldon patients

  • Osteopathy — Hands-on care for back, neck, joint and sports pain.
  • Physiotherapy — HCPC-registered chartered physio for injury, surgery recovery and chronic pain.
  • Sports Massage — Recovery, mobility and performance massage for active bodies.
  • Medical Acupuncture — Western medical acupuncture & dry needling for pain.
  • 1:1 Pilates — Private clinical Pilates tailored to your body.
  • Strength & Conditioning — Coached rehab and progressive loading to make you bulletproof.

Common Sheldon patient concerns

  • Office-related back and neck pain
  • Sports injuries — running, golf, rugby, tennis, gym
  • Post-surgical rehab and return to fitness
  • Pregnancy-related musculoskeletal issues
  • Pain that's been brushed off elsewhere

Getting to us from Sheldon

The clinic is at 114 Kineton Green Road, Olton, Solihull, B92 7EE — about 2.5 miles from Sheldon. There's free on-street parking outside, and we're a ten-minute walk from Olton railway station with the A12 bus stopping nearby.
