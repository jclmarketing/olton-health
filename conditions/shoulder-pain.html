Shoulder Pain

Shoulder Pain treatment, done properly.

Shoulders are the most mobile joints in the body — and the most easily upset. Rotator cuff problems, frozen shoulder and impingement are common in over-40s, swimmers, climbers and desk workers.

Book online Call 0121 314 0666

Rotator cuff problems

Pain on lifting the arm, weakness, night pain. Usually responds well to graded loading combined with hands-on work — surgery is rarely needed.

Frozen shoulder

A frustrating condition with three phases. We help you move through them faster with manual therapy, gentle stretching and acupuncture.

Impingement

Pain in the outer shoulder when reaching overhead. We work on shoulder blade control, rotator cuff strength and posture.
